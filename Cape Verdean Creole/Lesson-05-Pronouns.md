# Lesson 5 – People & Pronouns

## Learning Goal

Today you will learn how to:

- Say I, you, he, she, we and they
- Make simple sentences
- Talk about people

---

# Useful Sentences

## 1. I am Pedro.

**Creole:** N sta Pedro.

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

n stah PEH-droo

**Meaning:**

I am Pedro.

---

## 2. You are Ana.

**Creole:** Bu sta Ana.

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

boo stah AH-nah

**Meaning:**

You are Ana.

---

## 3. He is my friend.

**Creole:** El li nha amigu.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| El | He | ell |
| li | is | lee |
| nha | my | nyah |
| amigu | friend | ah-MEE-goo |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

ell lee nyah ah-MEE-goo

**Meaning:**

He is my friend.

---

## 4. She is my teacher.

**Creole:** El li nha prufesor.

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

ell lee nyah proo-feh-SOR

**Meaning:**

She is my teacher.

---

## 5. We are friends.

**Creole:** Nu li amigu.

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

noo lee ah-MEE-goo

**Meaning:**

We are friends.

---

# Conversation

## Ana

**Creole:** Bu sta Maria?

**Pronunciation:** boo stah mah-REE-ah

**Meaning:** Are you Maria?

---

## Pedro

**Creole:** Sin. N sta Maria.

**Pronunciation:** seen. n stah mah-REE-ah

**Meaning:** Yes. I am Maria.

---

## Ana

**Creole:** Prazer!

**Pronunciation:** prah-ZEHR

**Meaning:** Nice to meet you!

---

# Grammar Tip

Pronouns tell us **who** we are talking about.

| Creole | English |
|---------|----------|
| N | I |
| Bu | You |
| El | He / She |
| Nu | We |
| Es | They |
