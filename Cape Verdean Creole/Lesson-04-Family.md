# Lesson 4 – My Family

## Learning Goal

Today you will learn how to:

- Talk about your family
- Say who your family members are
- Ask if someone has brothers or sisters
- Count family members using numbers you already know

# Useful Sentences

## 1. This is my family.

**Creole:** Es li nha família.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Es | This | ess |
| li | is | lee |
| nha | my | nyah |
| família | family | fah-MEE-lee-ah |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

ess lee nyah fah-MEE-lee-ah

**Meaning:**

This is my family.

---

# Grammar Tip

**nha** means **my**.

Examples:

- nha mama = my mother
- nha papa = my father
- nha família = my family
