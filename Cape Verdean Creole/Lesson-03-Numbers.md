# Useful Sentences

## 1. How many?

**Creole:** Kuantu?

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Kuantu | How many | KWAHN-too |

**Sentence Pronunciation:** KWAHN-too

**Meaning:** How many?

---

## 2. One apple.

**Creole:** Um maçã.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Um | One | oom |
| maçã | Apple | mah-SAH |

**Sentence Pronunciation:** oom mah-SAH

**Meaning:** One apple.

---

## 3. Two books.

**Creole:** Dos livru.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Dos | Two | dohs |
| livru | Book | LEE-vroo |

**Sentence Pronunciation:** dohs LEE-vroo

**Meaning:** Two books.

---

## 4. Five friends.

**Creole:** Sinku amigu.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Sinku | Five | SEEN-koo |
| amigu | Friend | ah-MEE-goo |

**Sentence Pronunciation:** SEEN-koo ah-MEE-goo

**Meaning:** Five friends.

# Conversation

## Ana

**Creole:** Kuantu livru bu ten?

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Kuantu | How many | KWAHN-too |
| livru | Books | LEE-vroo |
| bu | you | boo |
| ten | have | ten |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**
KWAHN-too LEE-vroo boo ten?

**Meaning:**
How many books do you have?

---

## Pedro

**Creole:** N ten sinku livru.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| N | I | n |
| ten | have | ten |
| sinku | five | SEEN-koo |
| livru | books | LEE-vroo |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**
n ten SEEN-koo LEE-vroo

**Meaning:**
I have five books.

---

# Grammar Tip

Numbers come before the noun, just like in English.

Examples:

- Um livru = One book
- Dos livru = Two books
- Sinku amigu = Five friends
