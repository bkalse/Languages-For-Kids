# Lesson 1 – Greetings

## Learning Goal

Today you will learn how to:

- Say hello
- Greet people at different times of the day
- Ask "How are you?"

# Useful Sentences

## 1. Hello!

**Creole:** Oi!

**Pronunciation:** oy

**Meaning:** Hello!

---

## 2. Good morning!

**Creole:** Bon dia!

**Pronunciation:** bon dee-ah

**Meaning:** Good morning!

---

## 3. How are you?

**Creole:** Modi ki bu sta?

**Pronunciation:** MOH-dee kee boo stah

**Meaning:** How are you?

### Word Breakdown

- **Modi** = How
- **ki** = (question word connector)
- **bu** = you
- **sta** = are

---

## 4. I am fine.

**Creole:** N sta dretu.

**Pronunciation:** n stah DREH-too

**Meaning:** I am fine.

### Word Breakdown

- **N** = I
- **sta** = am
- **dretu** = fine / okay

---

## 5. Thank you.

**Creole:** Obrigadu.

**Pronunciation:** oh-bree-GAH-doo

**Meaning:** Thank you.

---

## 6. See you later.

**Creole:** Ti dipôs.

**Pronunciation:** tee dee-POHS

**Meaning:** See you later.

---

## 7. Goodbye!

**Creole:** Tchau!

**Pronunciation:** chow

**Meaning:** Goodbye!

---
