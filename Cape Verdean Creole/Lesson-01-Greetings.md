# Lesson 1 – Greetings

## Vocabulary

| English | Creole | Pronunciation |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| Hello | Oi | oy |
| Good morning | Bon dia | bon dee-ah |
| Good afternoon | Bon tardi | bon tar-dee |
| Good night | Bon noti | bon no-tee |
| Goodbye | Tchau | chow |
| Please | Pur favor | poor fa-vor |
| Thank you | Obrigadu (male) / Obrigada (female) | oh-bree-ga-doo |

## Useful Sentences

- Oi!
- Bon dia!
- Modi ki bu sta?
- N sta dretu.
- Obrigadu!
- Ti dipôs.
- Tchau!

## Conversation

Ana: Oi!

Pedro: Oi!

Ana: Modi ki bu sta?

Pedro: N sta dretu. Obrigadu!

Ana: Tchau!

Pedro: Tchau!

## Practice

Repeat each sentence three times.
