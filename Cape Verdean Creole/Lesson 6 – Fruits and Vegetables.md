# Useful Sentences

## 1. What is this?

**Creole:** Ki es li?

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Ki | What | kee |
| es | this | ess |
| li | is | lee |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

kee ess lee

**Meaning:**

What is this?

---

## 2. This is an apple.

**Creole:** Es li maçã.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Es | This | ess |
| li | is | lee |
| maçã | apple | mah-SAH |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

ess lee mah-SAH

**Meaning:**

This is an apple.

---

## 3. I like bananas.

**Creole:** N gosta di banana.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| N | I | n |
| gosta | like | GOS-tah |
| di | of | dee |
| banana | banana | bah-NAH-nah |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

n GOS-tah dee bah-NAH-nah

**Meaning:**

I like bananas.

---

## 4. I eat carrots.

**Creole:** N ta kume senora.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| N | I | n |
| ta | (present tense marker) | tah |
| kume | eat | KOO-meh |
| senora | carrot | seh-NOH-rah |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

n tah KOO-meh seh-NOH-rah

**Meaning:**

I eat carrots.

---

## 5. Two oranges.

**Creole:** Dos laranja.

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Dos | Two | dohs |
| laranja | oranges | lah-RAN-zhah |

**Sentence Pronunciation:**

dohs lah-RAN-zhah

**Meaning:**

Two oranges.

**Pronunciation:** boo GOS-tah dee MAHN-gah

**Meaning:** Do you like mangoes?

---

## Pedro

**Creole:** Sin! N gosta di manga.

**Pronunciation:** seen! n GOS-tah dee MAHN-gah

**Meaning:** Yes! I like mangoes.

# Grammar Tip

To say **"I like..."**, use:

**N gosta di...**

Examples:

- N gosta di banana.
- N gosta di maçã.
- N gosta di manga.
