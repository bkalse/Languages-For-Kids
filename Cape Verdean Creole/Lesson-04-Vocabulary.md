# Vocabulary

| English | Creole | Pronunciation | Word Sounds Like |
|----------|---------|---------------|------------------|
| Hello | Oi | oy | Like "boy" without the **b** |
| Good | Bon | bon | Rhymes with "gone" |
| Morning | Dia | dee-ah | dee + ah |
| Goodbye | Tchau | chow | Like "chow" |
| Please | Pur favor | poor fa-VOR | Stress on "vor" |
| Thank you | Obrigadu | oh-bree-GAH-doo | Stress on "GAH" |

---

# Word Pronunciation

## Oi

Pronunciation: **oy**

Sounds like: **boy** without the **b**

Meaning: Hello

---

## Bon

Pronunciation: **bon**

Sounds like: **gone**

Meaning: Good

---

## Dia

Pronunciation: **dee-ah**

Sounds like: **Dee + ah**

Meaning: Morning / Day

---

## Obrigadu

Pronunciation: **oh-bree-GAH-doo**

Sounds like: **oh + bree + GAH + doo**

Meaning: Thank you

---

## Modi ki bu sta?

### Word-by-word

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Modi | How | MOH-dee |
| ki | (question word) | kee |
| bu | you | boo |
| sta | are | stah |

Sentence Pronunciation:

**MOH-dee kee boo stah**

Meaning:

**How are you?**

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| N | I | n |
| sta | am | stah |
| dretu | fine | DREH-too |

Sentence Pronunciation:

**n stah DREH-too. oh-bree-GAH-doo**
